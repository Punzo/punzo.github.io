Davide Punzo

Software Developer

Davide Punzo is a scientific visualization software developer at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute. The Davide's investigation regards the use of quantitative and comparative 3-D visualization for inspecting the neutral hydrogen (HI) signal, i.e. the gas component, of galaxies. Davide is currently working mainly on software development related to the SlicerAstro project. His working interests range on technology, computational sciences and their applications both inside and outside astronomy.
